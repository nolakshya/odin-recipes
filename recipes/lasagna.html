LASAGNA

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering sheets of pasta with rich and savory fillings, then baking it to perfection. The layers typically consist of a hearty meat or vegetable sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and a generous topping of mozzarella or Parmesan, creating a combination of textures and flavors. Its golden, bubbly crust and the warm, comforting aroma make it a beloved meal for family gatherings and celebrations.

What makes lasagna truly special is its versatility. From the traditional meaty Bolognese version to vegetarian and even vegan variations, lasagna adapts to suit every palate. The dish is a symbol of comfort and indulgence, often served as a main course with a side of garlic bread or a fresh salad. Its hearty and satisfying nature makes it a favorite for cozy dinners and special occasions alike.

Ingredients

  • Meat: This super meaty lasagna has sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and garlic: An onion and two cloves of garlic are cooked with the meat to add tons of flavor.
  • Tomato products: You'll need a can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste.
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • Spices and seasonings: This lasagna recipe is flavored with fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles: Use store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles.
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese make this lasagna extra decadent.
  • Egg: An egg helps bind the ricotta so it doesn't ooze out of the lasagna when you cut into it.

STEPS

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic in a Dutch oven over medium heat until well browned.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Simmer, covered, for about 1 ½ hours, stirring occasionally.
  4. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C)
  7. To assemble, spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 ½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  8. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes.
  10. Rest lasagna for 15 minutes before serving.
