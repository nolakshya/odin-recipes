PASTA

Description

White sauce pasta is a creamy and indulgent dish that has become a favorite among pasta lovers worldwide. The key to its rich flavor lies in the luscious white sauce, also known as béchamel, made by blending butter, flour, and milk into a smooth and velvety base. The pasta, often penne or fettuccine, is cooked to al dente perfection and then tossed in the sauce, which is seasoned with salt, pepper, and a hint of nutmeg for depth. Grated cheese, like Parmesan, is often added for an extra layer of creaminess, while sautéed vegetables like broccoli, bell peppers, or mushrooms provide balance and color.

This dish is not only comforting but also incredibly versatile. You can elevate it by adding grilled chicken, shrimp, or even herbs like parsley and oregano for an aromatic touch. White sauce pasta is perfect for a cozy dinner or a special occasion, offering a restaurant-quality experience right at home. Its creamy texture, combined with the mild flavors of the sauce, makes it an instant crowd-pleaser for all ages.th a side of garlic bread or a fresh salad. Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons butter/li>
  • 2 tablespoons margarine
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon, crumbled
  • 1 ½ cups boiling water
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • ground white pepper to taste

STEPS

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Melt butter and margarine in a saucepan over medium-low heat.
  3. Add flour and crumbled bouillon; stir until well blended, 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Continue to cook and stir until thickened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
  5. Increase the heat to medium and whisk in water until smooth.
  6. Stir in milk; cook and stir until hot and thickened, 2 to 3 more minutes.
  7. Season with white pepper..
