NACHOS

Description

Nachos are a beloved Tex-Mex dish that brings together a delightful combination of flavors and textures. Typically made with crispy tortilla chips as the base, nachos are topped with melted cheese, creating a warm and gooey layer of deliciousness. This classic snack is often enhanced with a variety of toppings like jalapeños, beans, salsa, guacamole, sour cream, and sometimes seasoned meat or grilled vegetables, making it versatile and customizable to individual tastes. Whether served as a quick snack or a hearty meal, nachos are a crowd-pleaser that effortlessly balances crunchiness with rich and creamy toppings.

Perfect for sharing, nachos are a popular choice at parties, game nights, and casual gatherings. Their simplicity in preparation and the ability to cater to different preferences make them a favorite among both kids and adults. The dish's vibrant presentation, with colorful toppings layered over golden chips, adds to its appeal. Whether enjoyed as a quick bite at home or as a loaded platter at a restaurant, nachos offer a delightful experience that's equal parts comfort food and culinary adventure.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons canola oil
  • 6 green onions, sliced, white parts and tops separated
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1 shredded, cooked, whole chicken breast
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup salsa
  • ½ (12 ounce) package tortilla chips
  • 1 (8 ounce) package shredded Cheddar/Monterey Jack cheese blend
  • ½ large tomato, diced

STEPS

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Heat oil in a deep 12-inch skillet over medium heat. Add white parts of the green onions and garlic; cook and stir until onions are just tender.
  3. Mix in shredded chicken, salt, and pepper; toss until well coated. Stir in salsa.
  4. Arrange tortilla chips on a large baking sheet. Spoon chicken mixture over tortilla chips; top with shredded cheese and tomato.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese has melted, about 10 minutes. Remove from heat and sprinkle with green onion tops before serving..
